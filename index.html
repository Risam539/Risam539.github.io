Your clothes matter!

Style is key

Finding ones self is partly finding your style. 

When one finds their style they find themselves.

I believe that by having a good fashion sense, one can feel more confident and find themselves in a happier state of mind.

Fashion can impact your mood!

When you wear something preppy, you most likely feel confident and are more productive. When you wear cozy clothes you are less productive because you feel lazy.

Being fashionable is not a bad thing.

Many say fashion doesn't matter or have any impact on you or other people. Those who have a good fashion sense are noticed more and can be defined as people who well put together. People are more attracted to others who have similar styles as they do. Notice how friends all wear similar clothing! Fashion does impact you and other people.
